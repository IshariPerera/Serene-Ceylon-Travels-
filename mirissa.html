Why visit Colombo?

Marissa is the idyllic place for beach lovers. A golden slice of heaven located on the Southern tip of Sri Lanka! If you are planning to head down South, be sure you spend a few days in this charming seaside town. Mirissa is located just a few minutes away from Weligama. Although the vibe is a bit more mellow, we think Mirissa is perfect for anyone looking to spend their days with sandy toes and salty hair.



How to Get There

The best way to get to Mirissa is either by car, Tuk Tuk, or local bus. It is only about 15 minutes drive from Weligama, so if you are coming from that end, any of the above will suffice. If you are headed to Mirissa from Yala National Park, you do have the option of local bus, or car. We took a car, although a bit more expensive, it was easier for us and more comfortable.
